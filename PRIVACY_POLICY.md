## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

Your information is stored securely using:

- **Firebase Firestore** - cloud database for user profiles, projects, and tasks
- **Firebase Storage** - cloud storage for profile photos and media
- **Firebase Authentication** - secure credential management
- **Local Device Storage** - cached data via AsyncStorage (Secure Enclave for iOS, KeyStore for Android)

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption in Transit** - SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
- **Encryption at Rest** - data encrypted when stored in Firebase
- **Authentication** - secure password hashing and authentication protocols
- **Access Controls** - role-based access control (Admin, Manager, Employee)
- **Firewall Protection** - Firebase security rules to prevent unauthorized access
- **Regular Updates** - security patches and dependency updates
- **Monitoring** - continuous monitoring for suspicious activity

### 4.3 Data Retention

- **Active Accounts** - data is retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts** - all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Backup Data** - backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 Access Your Data

You have the right to access your personal information. Contact us with your request, and we will provide you with a copy of your data within 30 days.

### 6.2 Correct Your Data

You can update your profile information directly within the App. Navigate to your Profile screen to edit:

- Employment information
- Contact information

### 6.3 Delete Your Data

You can delete your account and all associated data through the App settings. This will:
- Delete your user profile
- Delete all your projects and associated tasks
- Delete your banking and employment information
- Delete your authentication credentials
- Remove all backups within 90 days

**Note:** This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

### 6.4 Data Portability

Upon request, we can provide your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format (JSON/CSV). Contact us with your request.

- **Marketing Communications** - opt-out by replying to emails with "UNSUBSCRIBE"

- **Analytics** - opt-out by disabling analytics in device settings (if available)

### 6.6 Withdraw Consent

For any processing based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time by contacting us.
